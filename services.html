Project Analysis
& Stimulation


When we receive a new job request, our first step is to conduct a comprehensive analysis. Our team assesses the job requirements, scope, and complexity to determine if we have the capabilities to undertake it successfully.

Then we ensure that we make informed decisions, we bring together our experienced team members who possess the relevant expertise. We conduct detailed discussions, sharing insights and perspectives to evaluate the potential complications and challenges associated with the job.

Then comes Megma Stimulation, a cutting-edge technique, involves simulating various failure scenarios using advanced modeling and simulation tools. This approach enables us to predict and understand the potential effects of failures more accurately, going beyond traditional methods of analysis.

Material Capability &
Castings


We possess the expertise and capabilities to provide a wide range of Cast Iron and Ductile Iron grades. Our comprehensive offerings include:

  • Cast iron: 150-250, 200-300, 250-350, 300-400.
  • Ductile Iron: 400/15, 400/12, 400/18, 500/7,600/3.
  • Aluminium: LM 4, LM 6, LM 25, ADC 12
  • Alubronze: All Grade.
  • Copper & Brass: All grade. Stainless steal: All Grade.

We take pride in delivering the best casting quality in the industry. With over 130 years of experience and a highly skilled team, we guarantee that every casting we produce will meet the highest standards.
